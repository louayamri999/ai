The ABCDE Guide of Melanoma Identification

Educate patients to perform routine body checks. Look for the following clinical warnings:

A
Asymmetry

One half of the spot or mole does not match the other half in shape.

B
Border

The edges are irregular, ragged, notched, blurred, or poorly defined.

C
Color

The color is not uniform; it includes shades of brown, black, red, white, or blue.

D
Diameter

The spot is larger than 6 millimeters across (about the size of a pencil eraser).

E
Evolving

The mole is changing in size, shape, color, elevation, or starts bleeding or itching.
